# DISPUTE_STRATEGY_SPEC.md

## Purpose
Define how KWANUS decides:
- what to dispute
- in what order
- why

---

## Core Principles

1. Not all negatives are equal
2. Order matters more than volume
3. Over-disputing reduces effectiveness
4. Strategy must feel intentional, not random

---

## Scoring Factors

Each item is scored based on:

- severity (impact on profile)
- recency
- balance
- type (collection, charge-off, etc.)
- data inconsistency
- bureau variation
- likelihood of success (estimated)

---

## Priority Rules

### Round 1
- 3-5 highest leverage items only
- focus on:
  - major impact
  - high inconsistency
  - strong dispute basis

### Round 2+
- follow-up items
- newly surfaced issues
- escalations if needed

---

## Dispute Reasons

System selects from:
- inaccurate information
- incomplete data
- unverifiable account
- outdated information
- personal info mismatch

---

## Output

For each item:
- why it was selected
- why now
- expected impact
- generated letter

---

## Guardrail
Never:
- recommend mass disputes
- suggest spam behavior
- create unrealistic expectations
