# HIMALAYA — Canonical Vision

## Identity

Himalaya is a **guided business operating system**.

It exists to take people from confusion to clarity, from scattered to structured, from stuck to moving — by combining intelligent diagnosis with strategic generation.

## The Problem

People trying to start or grow a business face:

- Too many tools with no guiding logic
- AI that generates without understanding context
- Platforms that treat beginners and operators the same
- No system that connects front-end (site, ads, content) to back-end (CRM, automation, follow-up, retention)
- Information overload with no clear sequence

## The Solution

Himalaya provides guided transformation through one sequence:

```
Entry → Diagnosis → Strategy → Generation → Optimization
```

### Entry
User enters the system with minimal friction. Himalaya determines their stage and situation before anything else happens.

### Diagnosis
Before generating anything, Himalaya deeply understands: what the person does, who they serve, what they sell, their stage, their bottlenecks, their strengths, and their gaps. If they have existing assets, Himalaya scans and scores them.

### Strategy
Himalaya decides what should happen next. What to build first. What not to build yet. What matters most for growth right now. This is the brain — the shortest path engine.

### Generation
Once strategy is clear, Himalaya generates the actual assets: site copy, page structure, funnel architecture, ad angles, email sequences, follow-up systems, SOPs, workflows, CRM pipelines. Always after diagnosis and strategy, never before.

### Optimization
After things are built, Himalaya helps improve them over time. It identifies weak points, suggests improvements, regenerates better versions, ranks changes by impact, and explains why each change matters.

## What Himalaya Is Not

- Not just tools (it thinks in outcomes)
- Not just generation (it diagnoses and strategizes first)
- Not one-size-fits-all (it adapts to user stage)
- Not overwhelming (minimal input, smart defaults)
- Not disconnected (front-end connects to back-end)

## Two Modes

### Beginner Mode
Guide the user step by step. Reduce decisions. Use smart defaults. Explain context. Never make the user feel dumb.

### Operator Mode
Let experienced users move faster, customize deeply, skip guidance, access advanced controls.

## Outcome Standard

Himalaya succeeds when a user can say:
> "I know exactly where I am, what matters most, and what to do next — and the system built it for me."

## Business Models Supported (Roadmap)

Phase 1: All (generic foundation)
Future specialization:

- Consultants/coaches
- Agencies
- E-commerce
- Personal brands
- Info products
- Affiliate businesses
- SaaS
