## How Top Consultants Find Clients

### Cold Outreach — What Actually Works
- **Loom Video Audit** (3–8x higher response rate than cold email): Find a business with clear gaps, record 2–3 min Loom walking through their specific website/ad issues, subject line references exactly what you found. Not a pitch — a free demonstration of competence.
- **Cold Email Framework**: Specific observation → one problem noticed → one result for similar businesses → "Worth 15 minutes?" — 5–7 lines max
- **LinkedIn**: Comment on their content 3x before DMing. Open with observation about their business, offer something free first.

### Free Audit Strategy
**Why it converts (20–40% of pre-qualified leads):**
1. Reciprocity — gave something valuable first
2. Demonstrates competence before being paid
3. Makes problems concrete and urgent
4. Creates logical next step: you diagnosed it, you fix it

**Where to find audit targets:**
- Facebook Ad Library: Same creative running 6+ weeks = not optimizing
- GTmetrix / PageSpeed: Businesses with slow sites
- Google: Businesses not ranking for their own core terms
- Sign up to their email list — audit the welcome sequence

**Free audit gives 2–3 findings only** — enough to create urgency, not the full fix. Written as:
- "What we found"
- "What it's costing you" (quantified in $)
- "What we'd recommend"
- CTA to a call

## Full Audit Framework (What the System Must Generate)

### Website Conversion Audit
- Above the fold: headline clarity, CTA visibility, load speed (<3 sec), value proposition specificity
- Trust/social proof: testimonials above fold, specific outcomes not generic praise, logos/press
- Conversion architecture: logical flow, multiple CTAs, form friction, trust signals near CTA
- Mobile: 60–70% of traffic is mobile — separate review required

### Ad Performance Audit
**Meta:**
- Account structure (TOF/MOF/BOF separation)
- Audience overlap
- Creative fatigue (frequency >2.5 + declining CTR)
- Landing page match
- Retargeting existence
- Pixel firing correctly

**Google:**
- Search term reports (broad match bleeding)
- Quality scores (<5 = problem)
- Negative keyword depth
- ROAS by campaign

### SEO Audit
- Technical: crawlability, index status, Core Web Vitals, mobile, schema, duplicate content, broken links
- On-page: title tags, header structure, internal linking, content depth, E-E-A-T
- Off-page: domain authority, backlink quality, local citations
- Keyword gaps: what competitors rank for that client doesn't

### Email Marketing Audit
- Sequences that must exist: welcome (5–7 emails), abandoned cart (3 emails: 1hr/24hr/72hr), post-purchase, win-back
- Benchmarks: open rate 20%+, click rate 2%+, unsubscribe <0.5%
- Email should drive 20–30% of e-commerce revenue — most businesses leave this completely untouched

### Offer & Positioning Audit (Highest Leverage)
Using Hormozi-style offer scorecard:
- Dream outcome (is it compelling?)
- Perceived likelihood of achievement
- Time delay (how fast do they see results?)
- Effort/sacrifice required
- Score each 1–10 → gaps = recommendations

## Signs of a High-Value Consulting Target

**Digital signals the system should detect:**
- Same Facebook ad creative running 6+ months
- No retargeting campaigns visible in Ad Library
- Site loads >4 seconds on mobile
- No email opt-in or no follow-up sequence
- Not ranking for branded terms
- No Google Reviews or unanswered reviews
- Traffic declining (SimilarWeb)
- Social with no posts in 30+ days

**Business signals:**
- Offer not clearly differentiated
- High-ticket with no clear USP
- Seasonal business with no off-season plan
- Local business not dominating local search

## What Separates Top 1% (Build Into the System)

1. **Specific positioning**: "We help e-commerce brands doing $1M–$10M scale to $5M–$20M" not "we do digital marketing"
2. **Documented SOPs**: Every process is written down and repeatable
3. **Ruthless client selection**: Fire bad clients, enforce clear criteria
4. **Deep niche expertise**: Know one or two verticals better than anyone
5. **The first 30-day win**: Always find and deliver something measurable in the first month
6. **Insight engine**: Constantly generating new findings across client accounts
