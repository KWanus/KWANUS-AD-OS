## Validation Checklist (Before Spending Anything)

- [ ] LP conversion rate: 2–4% high-ticket, 4–8% supplements/digital, 10–20% tripwire
- [ ] Refund rate: under 10% digital, under 5% physical
- [ ] Vendor reputation: Google "[product] scam", check BBB/Trustpilot
- [ ] Commission structure: initial + upsell stack, calculate Maximum Allowable CPA
- [ ] Cookie duration: 30+ days minimum
- [ ] Affiliate resources: swipe copy, approved creatives, tracking pixels provided
- [ ] Backend email: does vendor attribute repeat purchases to themselves (steals your commissions)?
- [ ] Payment proof: confirm at least one payment before scaling
- [ ] Traffic source allowed: explicitly verify with affiliate manager
- [ ] Run $50–100 direct-link test first to establish baseline CVR

## Full Funnel Architecture

**Never send cold traffic directly to vendor page.**

```
Ad → Bridge/Presell → Email Capture (optional) → Vendor LP → Purchase
```

### Funnel Types

**Advertorial** (best for health/supplements)
- Looks like news article or blog post
- 800–1,500 words, story-based, ends with CTA
- 2–5x better than direct linking on cold traffic

**VSL Bridge** (best for MMO, financial, complex mechanism)
- 3–8 min video pre-sells before sending to vendor VSL
- "Before you watch the next video, let me tell you what happened..."

**Review Page** (best for SEO + organic)
- "I Tried [Product] for 90 Days — Here's What Happened"
- Captures "review" search traffic, satisfies buyer skepticism

**Quiz Funnel** (highest engagement — top 1% use this more every year)
- "What's Your [Niche] Type?" → 5–8 questions → result page pre-sells
- 40–70% quiz completion rate, 60–80% email capture rate from completers

**Email Capture Bridge** (builds the owned asset)
- Capture email before sending to vendor (40–60% of ad clicks)
- Own the subscriber even if they don't buy today
- This is what separates affiliates who build assets from those who just buy traffic

## Ad Strategy

### Platform Reality (2024–2025)

| Platform | Best For | CPM Range | Key Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| Facebook/Instagram | Health, finance, home services, 35+ demos | Higher (post-iOS14) | Reels/Story = lower CPM than feed |
| TikTok | 18–34, impulse, visual products | Lower | TikTok Shop affiliate program = massive organic play |
| YouTube | High-ticket, courses, financial | $5–15 pre-roll | Search-based ads capture bottom-funnel intent |
| Native (Taboola/Outbrain) | Health, financial, survival, conservative demos | $3–15 | Can spend $10k+/day at scale |
| Solo Ads (Udimi) | MMO, health, relationship | $0.40–0.80/click | Filter for 70%+ Tier 1 traffic |

### Testing Matrix (Never Test Multiple Variables Simultaneously)
- Week 1: Test 3 angles ($50–100 each) → find winner
- Week 2: Test 5 creatives within winning angle
- Week 3: Test audiences with winning creative + angle
- Week 4: Test landing page variations

### Scaling Rules
- Increase budget 20–30% every 3 days (larger jumps reset algorithm learning)
- Never put more than 40% of daily budget in one campaign
- Kill campaigns without positive ROI signal within 3x target CPA spend
- Creative refresh when frequency hits 2.5+ (fatigue zone)
- Retargeting = often 3–5x ROAS vs. cold traffic — always run it

## Email Sequences

### Welcome Sequence (Days 1–7)
| Email | Timing | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Email 1 | Immediate | Deliver lead magnet, set expectations, no pitch |
| Email 2 | Day 1 | Your story — pure rapport building |
| Email 3 | Day 2 | Education — the real problem mechanism |
| Email 4 | Day 3 | Case study/testimonial with soft CTA |
| Email 5 | Day 5 | Objection handling + direct offer pitch |
| Email 6 | Day 7 | Urgency/scarcity close — hardest pitch |

**Broadcast:** 3–5 emails/week active promo, 1–2/week maintenance. 70% value, 30% pitch.

### Subject Line Patterns That Work
- Curiosity: "The real reason you can't [desired outcome]..."
- News: "Did you see what happened with [topical thing]?"
- Pattern interrupt: "I was wrong about [thing I said before]"
- Social proof: "She lost 47 lbs doing this one weird thing"
- Personal: "I need to tell you something..."

### Re-engagement (trigger: no open in 60 days)
- Email 1: "Did I lose you?"
- Email 2: "Last chance to stay" + value reminder
- Email 3: "I'm removing you from my list" (reverse psychology — often highest re-engagement)
- After 3 no-responses: delete. Deliverability > list size.

### ESP Selection (Critical)
Most major ESPs ban ClickBank links. Use: **Aweber, GetResponse, Sendlane, ActiveCampaign** (affiliate-friendly). NOT: Mailchimp, ConvertKit (bans affiliate links).

## Copy Frameworks

### Headline Formulas
- Curiosity Gap: "Scientists Baffled By Simple [Ingredient] That [Result]"
- Contrary Claim: "Why Everything You Know About [Topic] Is Wrong"
- Specific Number: "How 47,293 People Used This [Mechanism] to [Result]"
- Permission: "How to [Outcome] Without [Common Objection]"
- Warning: "[Number] Warning Signs You Have [Problem]"

### Angle Types
- **Curiosity/Mystery**: Tease mechanism, build entire presell to the reveal
- **Pain Agitation**: 60% pain agitation, 40% solution (most affiliates invert this and fail)
- **Social Proof/Bandwagon**: "200,000 people in 47 countries..."
- **Revelation/Secret**: "What [industry] doesn't want you to know..."
- **Story/Transformation**: Hero's journey — bypasses skepticism via identification
- **Us vs. Them**: Create an enemy (big pharma, mainstream advice, etc.)

## Compliance

### FTC Requirements (Non-Negotiable)
- Disclosure must be clear, conspicuous, unavoidable — not buried in footer
- Video: disclosure at start AND verbally stated
- Testimonials: must include "results not typical" if results are unusual
- Earnings claims: must include income disclosure showing typical affiliate results

### Platform Survival
- Separate domains per offer — one ban doesn't kill everything
- Multiple ad accounts/BMs in different entities — have 3–5 ready
- Real FTC disclosures paradoxically outperform cloakers long-term (no account losses)
- Run operations through LLC — protects personal assets

## What Separates the Top 1%

1. **Speed**: "Interesting offer" → "campaign live" in 24–48 hours (vs. 2–3 weeks for average)
2. **Data interpretation**: Dayparting, device breakdown, angle-level attribution, cohort analysis
3. **Direct vendor relationships**: Private rates (50% better than public), first access to new offers, custom LPs
4. **Proprietary angles**: New mechanism, counterintuitive angle, new audience segment nobody is targeting
5. **Capital management**: NET7 payment terms, business credit cards for float, 60–90 days operating reserve
6. **System building**: By month 6, they have SOPs, VAs, media buyers, content creators — not solo grinding
