- product link
- website link
- ad link

---

### 2. Extraction Layer

- product info
- offer
- visuals
- messaging

---

### 3. Diagnostic Engine

- audience
- pain points
- offer strength
- conversion quality

---

### 4. Decision Engine

- Reject
- Test
- Scale

### 5. Strategy Engine
Determines:
- angle

- channel
- positioning

---

### 6. Asset Engine
Generates:
- landing pages
- ads
- emails

---

### 7. Workflow Engine
Creates:
- step-by-step execution plan

---

### 8. Review Engine
Checks:
- quality
- clarity
- consistency

---

### 9. Memory Layer
Stores:
- patterns
- results
- performance signals
