# Operator Mode Framework

## Purpose
Help the user (or SaaS user) find winning products and launch profitable campaigns fast.

---

## Ideal Use Cases
- affiliate marketing
- dropshipping
- launching own products
- testing new offers

---

## Input
- product link
- niche (optional)
- goal (sales / leads / traffic)

---

## System Output

### 1. Product Verdict
- Winner / Potential / Reject
- Confidence level

### 2. Why It Could Win
- top 3 emotional drivers
- problem being solved
- desire being triggered

### 3. Buyer Profile
- most likely customer
- age range, situation, motivation

### 4. Best Selling Angle
- the one angle to lead with first
- why this angle fits this audience

### 5. Ad Package
- 3–5 hooks
- 2 ad scripts
- creative direction (format, style, tone)

### 6. Funnel Structure
- landing page headline
- offer stack
- upsell idea
- CTA

### 7. Email Flow (Light)
- welcome email
- abandoned cart
- follow-up push

### 8. Execution Plan
- Day 1: what to do
- Day 2: what to test
- Day 3: how to optimize
- Scaling trigger: when to scale

---

## Focus
Speed. Profit. No fluff.

Every output must be actionable within 24 hours.
