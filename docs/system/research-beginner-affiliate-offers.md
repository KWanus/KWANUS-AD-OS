## Beginner Guardrails

- Never recommend paid traffic to Amazon Associates (24-hour cookie = not viable)
- Flag Digistore24 as ClickBank alternative if user mentions account issues
- For budgets under $10/day: recommend organic-first before paid ads
- MMO niche: higher refund rates — recommend 7-day testing window vs 3-day
- Kill ad after $50 spend with zero ATC — don't wait for more data

## Gravity Benchmarks (ClickBank)

- 100+: Proven, high competition, higher ad costs
- 50–99: Validated, some competition, good starting point
- 20–49: Early-stage, lower competition, higher risk
- Under 20: Unproven — test organically before paid spend
