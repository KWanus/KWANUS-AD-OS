# PROFILE_BUILD_SPEC.md

## Purpose
Define how KWANUS strengthens a user profile after or alongside cleanup.

---

## Core Truth
Removal alone does not create funding readiness.

KWANUS must guide:
- what to add
- when to add
- how to manage it

---

## Key Areas

### 1. Account Types
- revolving accounts
- installment accounts
- mix balance

---

### 2. Utilization
- keep usage low
- ideal ranges
- timing of payments

---

### 3. Account Age
- avoid unnecessary new accounts
- protect older accounts

---

### 4. Credit Limits
- strategic increases
- spacing between actions

---

### 5. Additions

System may recommend:
- secured cards
- starter cards
- primary tradelines
- authorized users (with caution)

---

## Timing Logic

Do NOT:
- stack multiple accounts too quickly
- apply randomly

Do:
- space actions
- build gradually
- align with readiness

---

## Output

User sees:
- what to add
- when to add it
- why it matters
- what happens if they don't

---

## Guardrail
Never promote:
- reckless credit behavior
- unnecessary accounts
- short-term tricks that harm long-term profile
