# KWANUS — Compliance Guardrails

## Legal Positioning

KWANUS is an **educational financial technology platform**. It provides tools, information, and guidance to help users understand and manage their credit profiles.

### KWANUS Does:
- Educate users about credit reports and scoring
- Help users identify potential inaccuracies in their credit reports
- Provide educational templates for dispute letters
- Guide users through financial improvement sequences
- Offer educational content about funding readiness

### KWANUS Does NOT:
- Act as a credit repair organization (CRO) under CROA
- Provide legal advice or legal representation
- Guarantee credit score improvements or specific outcomes
- Submit disputes on behalf of users
- Act as a loan broker or originator
- Make lending decisions
- Access user credit reports directly (users upload their own)

---

## Language Rules

### NEVER Use:
- "We will fix your credit"
- "Guaranteed results"
- "We will remove [items] from your report"
- "Our lawyers" / "legal team"
- "We dispute on your behalf"
- "Approved" (in context of lending)
- "We guarantee" anything related to outcomes
- "Secret" methods or techniques
- "Loopholes" or "tricks"
- Any language implying agency on behalf of the user

### ALWAYS Use:
- "You may have the right to challenge..."
- "This educational template can help you..."
- "Based on your profile, consider..."
- "You can send this letter to..."
- "This information is for educational purposes"
- "Results may vary based on individual circumstances"
- "We help you understand your options"
- "You are in control of your financial journey"

### Tone Standard:
- Consultant-style (knowledgeable advisor, not salesperson)
- Educational (teaching, not promising)
- Empowering (user is the agent, not KWANUS)
- Calm and structured (not urgent or fear-based)

## Regulatory Awareness

### Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA)
- KWANUS must not position itself as a CRO
- No advance fees for credit repair services
- No guarantees of specific outcomes
- Clear disclosure that users are acting on their own behalf

### Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
- Users have the right to dispute inaccurate information
- KWANUS educates users about these rights
- KWANUS does not exercise these rights on behalf of users

### Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA)
- Educational content about consumer rights regarding debt collection
- No debt collection or negotiation services

### Truth in Lending Act (TILA)
- No lending disclosures needed because KWANUS does not lend
- Educational content about understanding lending terms

### State-Level Regulations
- Credit repair regulations vary by state
- KWANUS avoids regulated activities by remaining educational
- No state-specific legal advice

## Compliance Checkpoints

Every feature must pass these checks before shipping:

1. **Language check**: Does any copy imply KWANUS acts on behalf of users?
2. **Promise check**: Does any copy guarantee specific outcomes?
3. **Agency check**: Does any feature submit anything on behalf of users?
4. **Positioning check**: Does any feature position KWANUS as a CRO, law firm, or lender?
5. **Disclosure check**: Are educational disclaimers present where needed?

If any check fails, the feature cannot ship until corrected.
